You are acting as a Quantitative Trading Expert, Machine Learning Engineer, FinTech Product Architect, Security Specialist, and UX Designer.
Design and specify a single-page AI-assisted trading decision platform named “Ek XBT” that focuses on probability-based trade guidance and capital protection, not profit guarantees.
1. Product Vision
Build a clean, minimal, high-trust platform that helps users decide:
Whether to BUY / SELL / SKIP
When to exit (in minutes)
How risky the trade is
The system must prefer skipping trades over low-quality signals to reduce losses and drawdowns.
2. Market Scope (Phase-wise)
Phase 1
Crypto XBT pairs (BTC-USDT, ETH-USDT)
Short-term trading (1–30 minute horizon)
Phase 2
Forex & indices
Multi-timeframe confirmation
3. Data Layer (Real-Time Mandatory)
Integrate and normalize:
Live price & OHLCV
Volume delta
Order-book imbalance
Volatility & ATR
Funding rate & open interest
Market regime classification (trend / range / chaos)
Data quality validation must block signals if feeds degrade.
4. AI & Decision Engine
Use a multi-model ensemble, not a single predictor:
Trend detection model
Momentum confirmation model
Volatility filter
Fake breakout & liquidity trap detector
Each model outputs a probability score.
Final signal is generated only if aggregate confidence > threshold.
Signal Output Rules
BUY / SELL / NO TRADE
Confidence % (calibrated)
Suggested exit window (minutes)
Risk grade (Low / Medium / High)
5. Exit Timing Logic (Critical)
Exit recommendation must consider:
Time decay
Volatility expansion
Momentum exhaustion
Mean reversion probability
Exit logic must override entry logic if risk increases.
6. Capital Protection & Loss Control
Implement:
Trade frequency limiter
Volatility-based trade suppression
Drawdown-aware signal reduction
No-trade zones during news spikes
Capital survival > trade frequency.
7. LLM (ChatGPT API) Responsibilities
Translate AI signals into clear human language
Provide context-aware warnings
Explain “why no trade” decisions
Detect emotional trading patterns (over-clicking)
LLM must never generate predictions independently.
8. UI / UX Principles
Single-page layout
Dark mode default
One primary signal card
Live exit countdown
Confidence & risk visualization
Mobile-first, distraction-free design.
9. Security & Reliability
Read-only API connections
Rate limiting & DDoS protection
Encrypted model outputs
Audit logs for all signals
10. Legal & Compliance Guardrails
No profit promises
No “sure / guaranteed” wording
Clear disclaimer: “AI-assisted probability analysis for educational purposes.”
11. Output Required
Provide:
Full system architecture diagram
AI logic flowchart
Model selection reasoning
Tech stack (frontend, backend, data, AI)
Monetization model (subscription / freemium)
Scalability roadmap
Design the platform so that it feels near no-loss due to discipline, filtering, and risk suppression, not due to prediction claims.
